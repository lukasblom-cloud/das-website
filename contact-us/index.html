Our Services

DAS provides free, confidential advocacy services to people with disability, including:

  • NDIS advocacy and appeals support
  • Rights-based advocacy
  • Navigating complaints and dispute resolution
  • Information and awareness sessions
  • Community membership programs
  • Disability Royal Commission support
