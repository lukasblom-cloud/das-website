Membership Application

Join Our Community

Become a member of the Disability Advocacy Service and join a community of people with disability advocating for their rights and wellbeing.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to free advocacy services
  • Participation in community events and awareness sessions
  • Regular updates on disability rights and advocacy
  • Connection with other people with disability
  • Support and information for navigating NDIS and other services

How to Apply

Membership is free and open to people with disability. To apply:

  1. Complete the membership application form below
  2. Provide your contact details and brief information about your advocacy interests
  3. Submit the form or contact us directly
  4. We will confirm your membership and provide you with more information
