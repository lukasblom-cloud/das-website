About Us

Our Mission

Disability Advocacy Service Inc. (DAS) is a free, confidential advocacy service dedicated to supporting individuals and families with a disability. We empower our clients to exercise their own rights in accordance with the Disability Advocacy Standards.

What Defines Us

We are committed to:

  • Social Justice: Advocating for equality and rights of people with disability
  • Person-Centered Service: Placing our clients' needs and preferences at the centre of everything we do
  • Empowerment: Supporting clients to participate in decisions affecting their lives
  • Confidentiality: Providing safe, secure, and private advocacy services
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming and supporting people of all disabilities and backgrounds

Our Services

DAS provides a range of advocacy services including:

  • NDIS advocacy and appeals support
  • Rights-based advocacy for accessing services and supports
  • Support navigating complaints and dispute resolution
  • Community awareness and information sessions
  • Membership programs for people with disability
  • Disability Royal Commission support and information
