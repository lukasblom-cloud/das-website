Client Referral Form

Refer Someone to DAS

If you know someone with a disability who could benefit from our free advocacy services, you can refer them to DAS.

Who Can Be Referred

DAS provides advocacy services to:

  • People with any type of disability (physical, sensory, intellectual, psychosocial)
  • Family members or carers supporting someone with a disability
  • People navigating the NDIS or other disability support systems
  • Anyone needing support to exercise their rights

How to Make a Referral

You can refer someone to DAS by:

  • Contacting us directly and providing their information
  • Completing a referral form (available online or in person)
  • Providing them with our contact details so they can self-refer

Submit a Referral

To refer someone to DAS advocacy services, please contact us. We will follow up with the person directly to discuss how we can support them.

Privacy & Confidentiality

All referrals are handled confidentially. We will not contact anyone without their consent or knowledge. The person you refer will be asked if they would like to work with DAS before we provide any advocacy support.
