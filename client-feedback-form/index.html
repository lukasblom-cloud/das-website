Client Feedback Form

Share Your Feedback

We value your feedback and use it to improve our services. Whether you have positive feedback or suggestions for improvement, we'd like to hear from you.

How to Provide Feedback

You can provide feedback about our services by:

  • Completing our online feedback form (coming soon)
  • Calling us at (08) 8953-1422
  • Emailing us at admin@das.org.au
  • Visiting us in person at 3/11 Railway Terrace, Alice Springs

What We Want to Know

Your feedback can include:

  • Your experience with our advocacy services
  • How satisfied you are with the support you received
  • Areas where we could improve
  • Positive feedback about what worked well
  • Suggestions for new services or programs

Submit Your Feedback

To submit your feedback, please contact us directly. Your feedback is confidential and helps us serve you better.

Phone: (08) 8953-1422
Email: admin@das.org.au
Address: 3/11 Railway Terrace, Alice Springs, NT 0870
